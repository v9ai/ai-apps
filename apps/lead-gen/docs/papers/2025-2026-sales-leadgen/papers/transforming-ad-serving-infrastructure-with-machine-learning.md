# Transforming Ad-Serving Infrastructure with Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence

**Authors.** Arun Z. Thomas

**Venue / year.** Technix International Journal for Engineering Research · 2025

**Links.** [DOI](https://doi.org/10.56975/tijer.v12i6.158471) · [source](https://doi.org/10.56975/tijer.v12i6.158471)

**Abstract.**

The integration of Machine Learning (ML) and Artificial Intelligence (AI) technologies represents a paradigm shift in modern advertising infrastructure, revolutionizing how advertisements are created, targeted, delivered, and optimized. This technical review examines the transformation of traditional rule-based systems into sophisticated decision-making platforms capable of real-time ad selection, dynamic bidding optimization, and automated campaign management. Advanced user segmentation techniques leveraging clustering algorithms and pattern recognition have enabled unprecedented precision in audience targeting, while creative optimization frameworks now automatically generate and test thousands of variations to maximize engagement. The document explores emerging frontiers including large language models for semantic content understanding, reinforcement learning frameworks for long-term optimization, and computer vision applications for creative assessment. Technical challenges addressed include data privacy engineering through federated learning and differential privacy, model interpretability solutions for regulatory compliance, and computational efficiency optimization to meet real-time bidding requirements. As privacy regulations evolve and traditional tracking mechanisms face restrictions, these advanced technologies become increasingly vital to maintaining advertising effectiveness while respecting user privacy concerns.
