# Artificial Intelligence for Demand Prediction and Revenue Optimization in B2B Sales

**Authors.** Anand Dulal

**Venue / year.** 2026

**Links.** [DOI](https://doi.org/10.2139/ssrn.6533359) · [source](https://doi.org/10.2139/ssrn.6533359)

**Abstract.**

In the B2B sales, accurate demand forecasting is essential in order to maximize inventory and revenue. The old approach is not effective in reflecting intricate trends across stores and products. In this research, the regression model is random forest, which is applied to estimate the daily sales based on the synthetic data that models 2 years of B2B transaction. The model produced RMSE = 57.50 and MAE = 48.38 which shows the validity in terms of following trends and seasonal variations. The model is effective as demonstrated through visualization of predicted and actual sales against sales made per day. Results show that AI-based forecasting can enhance the decision-making process of operations and maximization of revenues.
