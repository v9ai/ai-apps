# Bridging Enterprise IT and Emerging Technologies: How Dev Technosys Leverages Salesforce and AI to Drive Digital Transformation: A Review

**Authors.** Tarun Nagar, Yash Bansal

**Venue / year.** International Journal For Multidisciplinary Research · 2025

**Links.** [source](https://www.semanticscholar.org/paper/8ac96390282099008a8ed859cc687745d425de69)

**Abstract.**

The convergence of enterprise IT with new technology has reconfigured the world of business to increase productivity, improve accuracy and decision making, and support communication mobility.
Salesforce, with the lightning platform, is the world’s #1 CRM, a powerful environment that has transformed from a transaction-first CRM tool into an enterprise ecosystem capable of driving digital transformation across industries.
AI possesses transformative powers when combined with a platform like Salesforce, as it is a major player in generative AI, predictive analytics, natural language processing (NLP) and automation.
This review article looks at how the global IT services and software development company Dev Technosys brings together Salesforce and AI to revolutionize enterprise digital transformation.
The paper explores the history of Salesforce, the emergence of AI-first operating models and what we’ve learned from customers working on Salesforce Einstein and AI co-pilots. It also explores Dev Technosys’s AI development expertise, such as generative AI, LLM fine-tuning, RPA, and computer vision, and how these can be implemented for Salesforce ecosystems.
This paper critically discusses strengths, weaknesses and opportunities, focusing on what sets Dev Technosys apart and how it can support business organizations in realizing the most value from Salesforce-AI integration.
Recommendations include accelerator-tooling, ethical intelligence, industry-based solutions and strong change management processes. The results highlight that Dev Technosys is uniquely positioned to connect the enterprise IT with latest in AI solutions, making it a credible partner for digital transformation in the fast evolving technology world.
