# Intelligent Bank Customer Churn Prediction: An AI Ensemble Approach Using Neural Networks and TabPFN Foundation Models

**Authors.** Nauman Pathan, Vishwajeet Patil, Ayushi Pahadia, Smita Bharne

**Venue / year.** 2026 IEEE International Conference on Interdisciplinary Approaches in Technology and Management for Social Innovation (IATMSI) · 2026

**Links.** [source](https://www.semanticscholar.org/paper/7a20823da445f9cdda237f3e0d45bfd6003409d7)

**Abstract.**

Banking institutions need customer churn prediction to ensure their profits remain secure and to increase customer retention. The proposed paper introduces a new AI ensemble method that integrates Artificial Neural Networks (ANNs) with TabPFN base models to improve customer churn prediction in banks. We use a domain-specific learning approach that leverages the potential of a custom neural network, combined with the novel and highly effective generalization capabilities of TabPFN, a foundation model for tabular data introduced in recent work. With an ensemble system, the accuracy on the standard bank customer churn data stands at 91.3%, whereas with a single model method, the accuracy is at 68.8%. The system comprises processes for large-scale data preprocessing, weighted ensemble methods, and a professional dashboard for real-time analytics. The experimental findings demonstrate better performance than current techniques, with practical results of this achievement evident in banking customer retention processes.
