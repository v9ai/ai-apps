# MAXIMUM MARKETING WITH THE USE OF ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE TO GROW BUSINESS INCOME

**Authors.** Sayfullayeva Nargiza Akromovna, Muxamedjonova Fotima Baxodir qizi, Muxamedjonova Zuxra Boxodir qizi

**Venue / year.** 2025

**Links.** [DOI](https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.17558442) · [source](https://zenodo.org/records/17558442)

**Abstract.**

This article is dedicated to developing an entrepreneur's income using artificial intelligence in maximum marketing. The use of artificial intelligence in marketing is very relevant and is quickly becoming one of the most effective systems for developing the income of entrepreneurs, as artificial intelligence helps us automate repetitive tasks, personalize marketing efforts and make decisions faster. When it comes to increasing brand awareness, artificial intelligence can perform a number of different tasks, including: author personalization in abstracts, transition from chatbots and virtual analytics, predictive analytics, content, email marketing, traffic optimization (SEO), marketing in social media, programmatic advertising, online optimization in marketing, production voice testing, it tries to cover the main areas of use of artificial intelligence in A/B testing, fraud detection, market research, dynamic pricing. This article examines the effectiveness of using artificial intelligence to help marketers improve campaign conversion, improve performance, and optimize website performance. This helps automate repetitive tasks, personalize marketing efforts, and make decisions faster.
