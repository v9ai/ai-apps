# Procurement Management in B2B Hospitality

**Authors.** Amit Sharma

**Venue / year.** 2025

**Links.** [DOI](https://doi.org/10.4018/979-8-3693-9760-2.ch013) · [source](https://doi.org/10.4018/979-8-3693-9760-2.ch013)

**Abstract.**

Procurement management is crucial in the B2B hospitality sector, as it ensures efficient operations, cost-effectiveness, and the optimization of supplier relationships. This chapter examines the strategic procurement process within the hospitality industry, highlighting key aspects such as supplier selection, risk management, and sustainable procurement practices. It also examines how technology can enhance procurement efficiency, leveraging digital platforms, data analytics, and automation. By tackling significant challenges like supply chain disruptions, compliance issues, and ethical sourcing, this chapter offers a comprehensive guide to effective procurement strategies that cater to the evolving needs of the hospitality industry.
