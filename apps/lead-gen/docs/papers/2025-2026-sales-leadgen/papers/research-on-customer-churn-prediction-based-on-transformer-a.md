# Research on Customer Churn Prediction based on Transformer and CNN Fusion Model

**Authors.** Xiaoyan Liu, Jingliang Liu

**Venue / year.** International Conference on Reliability, Maintainability and Safety · 2025

**Links.** [source](https://www.semanticscholar.org/paper/ac39d5025f22bf8b8eb550bcb9fcf5f0598c3672)

**Abstract.**

In the context of intensified competition in the telecommunications industry, customer churn has become a key factor threatening the reliability of network system operations and service continuity. To address the limitations of traditional user churn detection models in capturing complex user behavior sequences, this paper proposes a hybrid deep learning framework based on Convolutional Neural Networks and Transformers (TFCNN). The proposed framework innovatively combines the local feature extraction of convolutional neural networks (CNN) with the global dependency of transformers through a hierarchical feature fusion mechanism, which can accurately identify whether users will be lost. At the same time, a cosine annealing algorithm is introduced during training to dynamically adjust the learning rate and prevent falling into local optima in the case of a few samples. Public data experiments have shown that the proposed hybrid model has significant advantages in accuracy metrics and good model generalization performance compared to traditional machine learning methods. Its accuracy in determining whether a user has left the network is as high as 98.2 %, providing reliable user information for operators.
