# Natural Language Processing (NLP) for Industry Applications Sentiment Analysis for Customer Feedback

**Authors.** Wasswa Shafik

**Venue / year.** 2025

**Links.** [DOI](https://doi.org/10.4018/979-8-3373-2008-3.ch008) · [source](https://doi.org/10.4018/979-8-3373-2008-3.ch008)

**Abstract.**

Natural Language Processing (NLP) is revolutionizing industry applications by enabling advanced analysis and understanding of textual data. Sentiment analysis, a key NLP technique, plays a crucial role in deriving actionable insights from customer feedback across various sectors. This study explores the application of sentiment analysis in processing customer reviews, surveys, and social media interactions to identify patterns, gauge satisfaction levels, and inform decision-making. We examine methodologies ranging from lexicon-based approaches to cutting-edge machine learning and deep learning models, highlighting their efficacy and challenges in real-world scenarios. Emphasis is placed on handling multilingual data, mitigating biases, and improving sentiment detection accuracy in diverse cultural contexts. Integrating sentiment analysis with industry processes, businesses can enhance customer experience, drive product innovation, and maintain a competitive edge. This work underscores the transformative potential of NLP in harnessing the voice of the customer for strategic advantage.
