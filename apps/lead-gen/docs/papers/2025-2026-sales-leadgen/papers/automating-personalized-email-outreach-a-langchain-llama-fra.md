# Automating Personalized Email Outreach: A LangChain–LLaMA Framework with ChromaDB and Groq Cloud

**Authors.** V. M, T. Bhavana, C. Pranavi, N. H. Sethu, K. Prashsanth

**Venue / year.** 2025 5th International Conference on Ubiquitous Computing and Intelligent Information Systems (ICUIS) · 2025

**Links.** [source](https://www.semanticscholar.org/paper/d020d32b1b029dd39ddd5ef1ff51b373202b5d87)

**Abstract.**

In the modern sales ecosystem, email outreach remains a cornerstone for lead generation and client engagement. However, manually crafting personalized cold emails at scale is time-consuming and prone to inconsistencies. This study presents an end-to-end intelligent Cold Email Generator leveraging LLaMA 3.1, a state-of-the-art large language model (LLM), to automate and optimize the generation of cold emails. The system integrates LangChain for modular prompt chaining, ChromaDB as a vector store for context-aware retrieval, and Streamlit for a user-friendly front-end interface. This architecture enables real-time, contextually relevant, and customizable email generation tailored to specific industries, roles, and use cases. Furthermore, by deploying the model through the Groq Cloud, the system ensures high-throughput inference without local computational overhead. This paper outlines the system architecture, implementation, and performance evaluation, demonstrating the model’s effectiveness in generating high-quality personalized outreach content. The proposed solution offers a scalable and domain-adaptable tool for B2B marketing teams, significantly improving operational efficiency and response rates in cold-outreach campaigns.
