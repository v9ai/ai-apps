**Abstract.**

The central argument of this paper is simple and its implications are radical: in complex B2B selling, awareness is not a preliminary activity that precedes the real work of account-based marketing. It is the foundational infrastructure on which every subsequent phase of the ABM programme depends. Without it, outreach underperforms. Without it, thought leadership lands in a vacuum.
