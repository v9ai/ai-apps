# Marketing Mavericks" Riding the Wave of Innovation

**Authors.** E, Padmini, R, Jarshini, R, Janani, S, Sadhana, K, Dhanabackiyam, B, Kalpana

**Venue / year.** 2025

**Links.** [DOI](https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.16931269) · [source](https://zenodo.org/records/16931269)

**Abstract.**

Abstract: 
Marketing innovation is increasingly recognized as a critical driver of business growth and competitive advantage in the contemporary dynamic marketplace. This study examines the impact of innovative marketing strategies on firm performance and customer engagement. The findings reveal that organizations implementing innovative marketing practices&mdash;such as data-driven personalization, immersive technologies, and sustainability-focused campaigns&mdash;experience significantly higher revenue growth and customer retention compared to those employing traditional methods. These strategies also contribute to substantial increases in market share and brand loyalty. Ultimately, the results underscore the necessity for fostering a culture of innovation within marketing teams and aligning marketing efforts with evolving consumer expectations and technological advancements.
