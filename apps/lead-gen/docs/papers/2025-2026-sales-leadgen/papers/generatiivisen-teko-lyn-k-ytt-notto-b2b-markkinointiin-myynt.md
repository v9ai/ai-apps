# Generatiivisen tekoälyn käyttöönotto B2B markkinointiin, myyntiin ja toimitusketjunhallintaan startup-yrityksessä tehokkuuden parantamisessa

**Authors.** Piispanen, Emilia

**Venue / year.** LUTPub (LUT University) · 2025

**Links.** [source](https://lutpub.lut.fi/handle/10024/170778)

**Abstract.**

Generative Artificial Intelligence (AI) and its use cases in organizations have gained interest among researchers and companies. Companies have been starting to utilize generative AI in different business areas and operations. Generative AI can also be used in small companies and startups because of its accessibility and versatility, providing more value through multiple possible use cases. The study is a qualitative case study. Data was gathered from the literature and internal semi-structured interviews of the case company. The interview coding followed an abductive coding approach. Themes were developed from the interview data and connected to the theoretical chapters of this research. The study examined generative AI implementation in B2B marketing, sales, and supply chain management in a renewable energy startup company operating in B2B markets. The study created a concrete and practical strategy for generative AI implementation for the case company. It also found use cases in marketing, sales, and SCM, where generative AI can be implemented to bring value and help the company. The study offered recommendations for generative AI tools/applications for the areas that were fit to the company’s resources, size, and AI maturity. The study also created a roadmap for generative AI implementation
