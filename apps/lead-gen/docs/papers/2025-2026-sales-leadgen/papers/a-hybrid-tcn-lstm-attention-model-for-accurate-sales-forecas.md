# A Hybrid TCN-LSTM-Attention Model for Accurate Sales Forecasting

**Authors.** Xiao Chen, Punyaphol Horata, K. Sunat

**Venue / year.** Proceedings of the 2025 4th International Conference on Cyber Security, Artificial Intelligence and the Digital Economy · 2025

**Links.** [source](https://www.semanticscholar.org/paper/aea84ad037ba2e12c71d3d6519eb5cf5e80879f3)

**Abstract.**

Deep learning methods have greatly enhanced time series forecasting in recent years. Although, forecasting agricultural prices remains particularly difficult due to their nonlinearity, volatile fluctuations, numerous influencing factors, and notable seasonal patterns. To solve these problems, this paper puts forward a new hybrid framework, TCN-LSTM-MLP-Attention, which integrates Temporal Convolutional Networks (TCN) for capturing multi-scale dependencies, LSTM for identifying long-range trends, MLP for nonlinear feature transformations, and an attention mechanism that emphasizes critical time steps. Experiments on the Hass avocado dataset indicate that the proposed model decreases MAE by 15.3% relative to a stand-alone LSTM and surpasses CNN-LSTM by 10.7%, while also achieving superior scores in MSE, RMSE, and R2.These gains underscore its utility in agricultural price forecasting and supply chain management.
