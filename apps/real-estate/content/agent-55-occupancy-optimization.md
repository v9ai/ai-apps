# Occupancy Prediction and Space Optimization: Research Landscape

## **1. Occupancy Prediction from Sensor Data**

### **Multi-Modal Sensor Fusion Approaches**
- **WiFi-based occupancy detection**: MAC address tracking, RSSI fingerprinting, device counting
- **Environmental sensors**: CO₂ concentration patterns, temperature gradients, humidity changes
- **Computer vision systems**: Camera-based people counting, thermal imaging, privacy-preserving approaches
- **Badge/access control data**: Swipe card analytics, door entry/exit patterns
- **IoT sensor networks**: PIR motion sensors, acoustic sensors, light sensors

### **Machine Learning Methods for Occupancy Prediction**
- **Time-series forecasting**: LSTM, GRU, Transformer models for temporal patterns
- **Classification models**: Random Forest, XGBoost for occupancy state prediction
- **Deep learning approaches**: CNN-LSTM hybrids for spatial-temporal patterns
- **Ensemble methods**: Combining multiple sensor modalities and algorithms
- **Transfer learning**: Adapting models across different building types

### **Key Research Challenges**
- **Privacy preservation** in occupancy monitoring
- **Sensor calibration** and data quality issues
- **Real-time processing** requirements
- **Model generalization** across different spaces
- **Energy efficiency** of sensor networks

## **2. Space Utilization Analytics**

### **Office Space Analytics Metrics**
- **Utilization rates**: Peak vs. average occupancy, space-time utilization
- **Density analysis**: People per square meter, circulation space efficiency
- **Space type analysis**: Meeting rooms vs. workstations vs. collaborative areas
- **Temporal patterns**: Daily, weekly, seasonal variations
- **Space adjacency analysis**: Proximity and interaction patterns

### **Coworking Space Analytics**
- **Member behavior patterns**: Regular vs. occasional users
- **Space preference analysis**: Quiet zones vs. collaborative areas
- **Resource utilization**: Meeting rooms, phone booths, amenities
- **Community interaction metrics**: Social network analysis within spaces

### **Analytics Platforms and Tools**

- **Open source frameworks**: Building analytics toolkits
- **Dashboard visualization**: Real-time occupancy maps, heat maps
- **Predictive analytics**: Space demand forecasting
