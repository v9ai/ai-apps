## Search Intent
This topic attracts technical founders, data scientists, and revenue operations professionals in small-to-mid-sized B2B companies or startups. They are searching for a cost-effective, privacy-conscious, and customizable alternative to expensive sales intelligence platforms like ZoomInfo. Their primary intent is to **learn** and **do**—they want a practical, step-by-step guide to building a functional prototype using specific open-source tools (Qwen for language understanding and MLX for efficient local inference). The outcome they desire is a working system that can process local data (e.g., company websites, news) to detect buying signals (like "looking for a solution" or "hiring a VP of Sales") without relying on a third-party SaaS. The best content format is a detailed technical tutorial with code snippets, architecture diagrams, and clear explanations of trade-offs.
