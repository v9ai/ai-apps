**Victor Cheng et al. (2020)** [Improving English Phoneme Pronunciation with Automatic Speech Recognition Using Voice Chatbot](https://doi.org/10.1007/978-981-33-4594-2_8). Demonstrates the integration of ASR with conversational agents for real-time pronunciation feedback.
