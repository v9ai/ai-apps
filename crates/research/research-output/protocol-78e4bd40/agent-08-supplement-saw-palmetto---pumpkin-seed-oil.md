# **Cognitive and Neuroprotective Effects of Saw Palmetto + Pumpkin Seed Oil (320 mg + 400 mg daily)**

## **2. Mechanisms of Action Relevant to Brain Health**

### **Primary Hormonal Mechanism**
**5α-Reductase Inhibition** [Strong]
- Both compounds inhibit 5α-reductase enzymes (types I and II)
- Reduces conversion of testosterone to dihydrotestosterone (DHT)
- **Neurosteroid Implications**: DHT reduction may affect neurosteroid metabolism, potentially influencing GABAergic and glutamatergic neurotransmission

### **Secondary Neuroprotective Mechanisms**

#### **Anti-inflammatory Effects** [Moderate]
- **Pumpkin Seed Oil**: Inhibits IL-6 activity in RAW 264.7 cells [W4288450193]
- **Saw Palmetto**: Reduces inflammatory markers in prostate tissue models
- **Brain Relevance**: Neuroinflammation reduction may protect against neurodegenerative processes

#### **Antioxidant Activity** [Moderate]
- **Pumpkin Seed Oil**: Rich in vitamin E, carotenoids, and phenolic compounds
- **Saw Palmetto**: Contains β-sitosterol and other phytosterols with antioxidant properties
- **Mitochondrial Protection**: Antioxidant effects may support mitochondrial function

#### **Neurotransmitter Modulation** [Emerging]
- **Acetylcholinesterase Inhibition**: Pumpkin seed components may inhibit AChE
- **Monoamine Effects**: Animal studies show elevated norepinephrine levels with pumpkin seed treatment

#### **Synaptic Plasticity** [Limited]
- Indirect effects via neuroinflammation reduction and oxidative stress mitigation
- No direct evidence on BDNF or synaptic protein modulation

## **3. Optimal Dosing for Cognitive Outcomes**

### **Current Evidence-Based Dosing**
- **Pumpkin Seed Oil**: 400 mg daily (based on hair growth studies) [W2143156312]
- **Saw Palmetto**: 320 mg daily (standardized extract)
- **Combination Rationale**: Synergistic 5α-reductase inhibition

### **Bioavailability Considerations**
- **Fat-Soluble Compounds**: Both are lipophilic, requiring dietary fat for optimal absorption
- **Standardization Issues**: Variability in active compound concentrations between products
- **Timing**: Should be taken with meals containing fat

### **Dose-Response Data**
**[Limited]** No dose-response studies for cognitive endpoints. Hair restoration studies show:
- Pumpkin Seed Oil: 400 mg/day showed 40% hair count increase vs. 10% placebo
