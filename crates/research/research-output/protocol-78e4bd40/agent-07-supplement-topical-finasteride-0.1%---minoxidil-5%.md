## **2. Mechanisms of Action Relevant to Brain Health**

### **Neurosteroid Pathway Disruption:**
- **5α-Reductase Inhibition**: Finasteride inhibits conversion of progesterone and testosterone to neuroactive metabolites (allopregnanolone, THDOC) [**Strong**]
  - Mendell & MacLusky (2018): Neurosteroid metabolites are neuroprotective
  - Maguire (2019): Neuroactive steroids and GABAergic involvement in depression
  - Rodriguez-Cerdeira (2026): 5α-reductase isozymes essential for neurosteroid biosynthesis

1. **Allopregnanolone**: Potent positive allosteric modulator of GABA-A receptors
   - Critical for mood regulation, stress response, neurogenesis
   - FDA-approved for postpartum depression (brexanolone)

2. **Dihydrotestosterone (DHT)**: Conversion from testosterone blocked
   - DHT has neuroprotective effects via androgen receptors
   - Cai & Li (2020): Androgens have neuroprotective effects and deficiency impairs cognitive function

### **Oxidative Stress and Mitochondrial Function:**
- **Indirect Effects**: Neurosteroid deficiency may increase oxidative stress
- **No Direct Evidence**: For topical formulation specifically
