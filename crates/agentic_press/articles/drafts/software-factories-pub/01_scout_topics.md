   Why it's trending: Humanitec released a benchmark report analyzing over 100 platform teams, revealing a counterintuitive finding that the most successful "golden paths" increase developer choice within guardrails, rather than restricting it.  
   Primary Source: [Humanitec State of Platform Engineering 2025: The Paved Road Paradox](https://humanitec.com/blog/state-of-platform-engineering-2025)

   Why it's trending: The latest Backstage community survey highlighted that the heaviest cost for adopters is not the initial setup, but the ongoing maintenance of tightly controlled software templates, sparking debate about template rigidity vs. composability.  
   Primary Source: [Spotify Backstage: 2025 Community Survey Results - The Cost of Control](https://backstage.spotify.com/blog/2025-community-survey-results/)

5. **CNCF's "Platform Engineering Metrics" Whitepaper and the DORA Misconception**  
   Why it's trending: The CNCF Platform Engineering TAG published a new whitepaper that clarifies a widespread misconception: that platform success is measured by *improving* DORA metrics for *all* teams, rather than *reducing the variance* of those metrics across teams.  
   Primary Source: [CNCF TAG Platform Engineering: Measuring Platform Success - Whitepaper v2](https://tag-platform.cncf.io/whitepapers/measuring-success/)
